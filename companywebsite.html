IVTL Infoview Technologies (Infoview) is a pioneer in providing IT services to customers across the globe with a focus on the Japanese market. The company was founded in 2000 in India (Chennai) and in 2001 in Japan (Tokyo).
It is a wholly owned subsidiary of Works Applications, Japan and Infoview provides design, development, implementation and support for Works Applications ERP suites (Human Resource, Accounting and Supply Chain).
With the combined strength of Works Applications, Japan we provide Development, Testing, Infrastructure, and consulting services to customers in various geographies.
